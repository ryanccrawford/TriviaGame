How to Play

From the 'Intro Screen':

  1. Press the 'Start Game' button. (There will be a short pause)
  2. A game timer and question box will display.
  3. Click on one of the choices below the question to answer the question

Read the question and before the timmer expires, pick one of the listed answers. If the timmer runs out of time before you select an answer, you will get the question wrong then move on to the next question until all questions are presented

If you answer incorrectly, the correct answer will display. If you get it correct you will be congradulated, then the game will continue until all questions are answered.

Finishing the Game

Once you finish, your game stats will be displayed to show you how well you did.

Click the back button to return to the previous screen.
